I'm a Staff Research Engineer at [Google DeepMind](https://deepmind.google/) and a doctoral researcher at [UCL](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/) under [John Shawe-Taylor](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/10973-john-shawetaylor). I work on AI systems and multimodal models that improve and adapt through interaction with the world.

Before AI, I worked on control theory and robotics. I did my Bachelor's at the [University of Sarajevo](https://www.unsa.ba/en) and Master's at [ETH Zürich](https://ethz.ch/en.html), with an external thesis at the [Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems](https://is.mpg.de/). In between, I spent time at [Rapyuta Robotics](https://www.rapyuta-robotics.com/) working on autonomous warehouse robots and end-to-end drone autonomy.

I founded [ANNT](https://annt.ba/) to help connect Bosnian science and tech talent worldwide. Last year I organised [EEML 2025](https://www.eeml.eu) in Sarajevo, with 350 participants from 50 countries, the largest in-person edition so far (the [wrap-up post]({% post_url 2025-08-08-eeml-wrap-up %}) has the full story).

I also love building tools, especially educational ones. Lately that has meant [Möbius](/mobius/), a personalized AI agent you self-host that builds the apps you ask for and the interface around them. A handful of others, both old robotics tools and newer experiments, live on the [projects page](/projects/).
